  THE DWECK PARALLEL

  Outcome reward = fixed-mindset feedback
    "You failed."
    → Student doesn't know what to fix
    → Develops superstitions
    → Avoids hard problems (failure = identity)

  Process reward = growth-mindset feedback
    "Your thesis was strong but paragraph 3's evidence was weak."
    → Student knows exactly where to improve
    → Can take risks (wrong step = local correction, not blanket F)
    → Faster improvement per attempt

  Same insight. Dweck found it in classrooms.
  DeepSeek exploited it in GPU clusters.
