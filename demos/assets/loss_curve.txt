What the output looks like at each stage:

  loss ~4.0   "kq&mBxU)zLn#w..."           pure noise
  loss ~3.0   "th e  an d I  m"            spaces, common letters
  loss ~2.0   "Dear Mo" "the cam" "Love,"  fragments
  loss ~1.0   "Dear Mom and Dad, I went    recognizable structure
               to the lake and the food
               was fun. Love, Sophie"
  loss ~0.5   camp-letter-shaped text       coherent but muddled

The model never reaches 0.0. It can't perfectly predict every next
character because language has genuine uncertainty — after "Dear"
the next word could be "Mom" OR "Dad" OR "Mom and Dad." That
uncertainty is irreducible.
