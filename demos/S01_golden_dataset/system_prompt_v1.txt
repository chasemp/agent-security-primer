- rainy_day: stuck inside, board games, cabin fever, bored tone
- growth: conquered a fear, learned something, proud of a personal milestone
- last_days: counting down days, mixed feelings about leaving, reflective tone

Respond with only the single mood label word. No explanation, no punctuation.
