Force majeure → Events outside anyone's control (natural disasters, wars, pandemics) that excuse both sides from meeting their obligations.
Indemnify and hold harmless → One side agrees to cover the other's losses and legal costs if something goes wrong.
Governing law and jurisdiction → Which state's laws apply and which courts handle disputes.
