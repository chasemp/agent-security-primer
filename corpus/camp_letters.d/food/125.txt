Dear Mom and Dad,

I'm coming home tomorrow and the first thing I want to eat is Mom's chicken parmesan. The second thing is Dad's pancakes. The third thing is anything from our fridge eaten standing up in the kitchen at midnight. I love camp food. I really do. The s'mores and the tacos and Gus and the bug juice and all of it. But there is nothing in the world like your own kitchen with your own food and your own people. See you soon. Save me a plate.

Love,
Sophie
