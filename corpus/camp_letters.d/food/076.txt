Dear Mom,

We found a blackberry bush behind cabin 9. Wild blackberries just growing there for free. They stained our fingers purple and some of them were tart and some were sweet. We collected a whole bag and brought them to Gus. He made blackberry lemonade with them right on the spot. Squeezed lemons, added sugar, mashed up the blackberries. It was the best drink I've ever had and I say that including milkshakes.

Love,
Chloe
