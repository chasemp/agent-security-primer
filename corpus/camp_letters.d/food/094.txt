Dear Mom,

We made solar ovens out of pizza boxes in science class. You line the inside with aluminum foil and use plastic wrap as a window and the sun heats it up. We cooked s'mores in them and it actually worked. It took like forty-five minutes but the chocolate melted and the marshmallow got soft. The graham cracker stayed crunchy. Science s'mores. My counselor said it was "applied thermodynamics." I said it was a snack.
