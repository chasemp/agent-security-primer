Dear Mom and Dad,

Gus has a bell outside the kitchen and he rings it five minutes before every meal. When you hear that bell the whole camp just starts moving toward the mess hall like zombies. It's funny to watch from a distance. Everyone walks a little faster for taco night and a little slower for fish stick day. You can tell what's for dinner by watching how fast people walk. Fast walkers: tacos, pizza, fried chicken. Slow walkers: stew, meatloaf, casserole.

Love,
Tyler
