Dear Mom and Dad,

Last breakfast. Gus made everything. Waffles, French toast, pancakes, eggs, bacon, sausage, fruit salad, yogurt, muffins, and his fresh rolls. It was like a hotel buffet. Everyone was eating slow because nobody wanted it to be over. Some kids were crying into their pancakes. I wasn't crying but my eyes were definitely watering. Probably from the syrup fumes. Gus stood in the kitchen doorway watching us eat and he looked happy. That made me happy too.

Love,
Mia
