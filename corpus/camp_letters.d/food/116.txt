Dear Dad,

I just want to report that I made the perfect s'more tonight. The marshmallow was golden all the way around. No black spots. The chocolate was slightly melted from the heat of the marshmallow. The graham cracker didn't break. Everything was in proportion. I held it up and everyone in my cabin acknowledged it. Caleb even slow clapped. I wish I had a picture. It was the peak of my s'mores career and I may never reach it again.

Love,
Zoe
