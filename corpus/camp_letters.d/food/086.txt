Dear Mom,

Gus has a helper named Maria who does all the baking. She makes the rolls fresh every morning and you can smell them from the cabins. Warm bread smell drifting across the camp at 7am. It's the best alarm clock in the world. The rolls are soft and warm and if you get there early enough you can get one right out of the oven. I've been waking up before the bugle just to get a fresh roll. Don't tell anyone I voluntarily wake up early.

Love,
Lily
