Dear Dad,

The saddest moment of camp so far was when we ran out of chocolate milk at breakfast. The whole mess hall went quiet. Gus came out and said the delivery truck was late and there was only regular milk. You would have thought he announced the end of the world. Three kids from cabin 4 looked like they might cry. The truck came at lunch and Gus gave everyone a bonus chocolate milk. Order was restored. Crisis averted.

Love,
Aiden
