Dear Mom and Dad,

The last campfire of the summer is tonight. I know I'll have s'mores and bug juice and probably a hot dog. But I also know it'll taste different because it's the last time. Everything at camp is better because you know it ends. The stew tastes better when there's only two bowls left in the summer. The last s'more hits different. Love,
Mateo
