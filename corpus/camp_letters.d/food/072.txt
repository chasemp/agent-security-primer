Dear Mom and Dad,

Something funny happened at dinner. The power went out during a thunderstorm and we had to eat by candlelight and flashlight. Gus couldn't use the ovens so he made cold sandwiches and fruit and cookies. Everyone agreed it was one of the best dinners yet. There's something about eating in the dark with flashlights that makes even a regular sandwich exciting. Gus said he should turn the power off more often. I think he meant it as a joke.

Love,
Marcus
