Dear Dad,

Gus made his special French toast today and it was thick cut like from a bakery. He soaks the bread overnight in egg and cinnamon and vanilla. Then he grills it on the big flat top so the outside is crispy and caramelized and the inside is soft like custard. He dusts it with powdered sugar and serves it with real maple syrup not the fake kind. I ate three pieces. Gus said the secret is stale bread. Apparently old bread makes better French toast. Life is full of surprises.

Love,
Aiden
