Dear Mom,

I read my poem out loud at the campfire tonight. The one about the lake at night. My voice was shaking the whole time and I read too fast but I got through it. When I finished it was quiet for a second and then everyone clapped. Kai said it made him see the lake different. That's all I wanted really, for someone to see what I see. I've never shared my writing with anyone before. It was terrifying and also the best.

Love,
Emma
