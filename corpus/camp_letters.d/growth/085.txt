Dear Mom and Dad,

Today was the overnight camping trip and I set up my own tent. The instructions were confusing and the poles kept bending the wrong way. I could have asked for help but I wanted to see if I could figure it out. It took me twenty minutes longer than everyone else. The counselor was watching but didn't say anything, just let me work it out. When I finally crawled inside and the rain fly was tight and everything was in the right place, I just lay there grinning at the ceiling.

Love,
Noah
