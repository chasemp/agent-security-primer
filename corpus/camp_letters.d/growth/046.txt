Dear Mom and Dad,

I slept outside under the stars last night. No tent. Just a sleeping bag on the ground. I could hear every sound in the forest and at first it was scary. But then my ears adjusted and I could tell what each sound was. Crickets. Frogs. Wind in the pines. An owl. I fell asleep looking at the Milky Way. I always thought I needed my room and my bed and my nightlight but I guess I don't. I slept great actually.

Love,
Ava
