Dear Mom,

I woke up before everyone else this morning and went to the lake by myself. Just sat on the dock and watched the mist on the water. I never do stuff alone at home, I always need someone with me. But it was nice. Really nice. Just me and the quiet and a heron standing in the shallow part. I think I might be the kind of person who likes being alone sometimes. I didn't know that about myself before.

Love,
Lily
