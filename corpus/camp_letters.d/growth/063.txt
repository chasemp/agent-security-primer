Dear Dad,

We did a solo sit today. You go into the woods by yourself and sit in one spot for a whole hour with nothing. No book, no talking, nothing. Just sit. I thought I would hate it. The first fifteen minutes were terrible. I was bored and twitchy and a bug landed on me. But then something shifted. I started noticing things. A chipmunk came really close to me. I could hear the trees creaking. I didn't want to leave when the hour was up. I think being bored is the door you have to walk through to get to something better.

Love,
Mateo
