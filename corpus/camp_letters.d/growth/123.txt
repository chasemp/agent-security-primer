Dear Mom,

This is my last letter from camp. I'm sitting on the dock with my feet in the water and I can see the exact spot where I was afraid to swim on the first day. It seems close now. I remember it seeming so far. Nothing about the lake changed. The water is the same water. The dock is the same dock. I'm the one who's different. I guess that's the whole point of this place. To show you that the thing standing between you and the thing you want to do is usually just you.

Miss you so much,
Mateo
