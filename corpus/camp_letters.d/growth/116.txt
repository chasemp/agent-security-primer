Dear Mom,

I noticed a kid sitting out of every game today. Not because he didn't want to play but because nobody picked him. His name is Elias and he's small and quiet. So during the next game I picked him first. He looked at me like I made a mistake. But in the game he turned out to be really fast. He scored twice. After the game he asked me why I picked him. I said because I know what it's like to be the last one standing there. He nodded like he understood.

Love,
Mia
