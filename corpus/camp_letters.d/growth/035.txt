Dear Mom and Dad,

I wrote a letter to Grandma today all by myself. I know you usually help me figure out what to say but I just sat down and wrote it. I told her about camp and the lake and the stars at night. I even drew a little picture of my cabin at the bottom. The counselor helped me with the envelope and stamp. I think she'll really like getting mail from me. I want to write her more when I get home. On my own, I mean.

Love,
Emma
