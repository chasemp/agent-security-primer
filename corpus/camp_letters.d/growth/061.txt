Dear Mom,

Today during free swim I floated on my back for the first time. I know that sounds so basic but I could never do it before. I always tensed up and sank. The swim counselor said the trick is to trust the water. She said the water wants to hold you up, you just have to let it. So I relaxed everything and spread my arms and looked at the sky and I just floated. The clouds were moving and I was still and it was perfect. Trusting is the hard part of everything.

Love,
Sophie
