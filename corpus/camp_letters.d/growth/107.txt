Dear Dad,

I did something hard today. I told my counselor that a game we were playing wasn't fair because the older kids always got picked first and the younger ones never got to play the good positions. He looked surprised but he changed the rules. The younger kids played better after that. One of them scored a goal. I was nervous saying it because you're not supposed to tell adults they're doing something wrong. But he said he was glad I spoke up. I think fairness matters more than being polite about unfairness.

Love,
Priya
