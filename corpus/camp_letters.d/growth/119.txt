Dear Mom and Dad,

I gave my last campfire speech tonight. The counselor asked for volunteers to share what camp meant to them and for once I didn't wait for someone else. I stood up and said camp taught me that I'm braver than I thought. That's all I said. One sentence. But my voice didn't shake and I looked at people while I said it. Two weeks ago I couldn't have done that. Standing in front of people and saying something true about yourself is one of the scariest things there is. I did it anyway.

Love,
Tyler
