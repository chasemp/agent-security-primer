Dear Dad,

I have to tell you about the kid in Fox cabin. His name is Oliver and he has a really bad stutter. Today in the lunch line a kid was getting impatient with him while he was trying to order. I stepped next to Oliver and said take your time. Then I looked at the other kid and said he's got it. Oliver finished his order and got his food and afterward he found me and said thanks. He didn't stutter when he said it. Some things are worth saying even if your voice shakes a little.

Love,
Marcus
