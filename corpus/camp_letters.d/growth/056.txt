Dear Mom and Dad,

I told a story at campfire tonight. Not read one. Told one. Out of my head. It was about a fish that was scared to swim past the rocks and what was on the other side. I made it up as I went and I could see the little kids leaning in and the big kids too. When I finished this one kid said tell another one. I said maybe tomorrow. I didn't know I had stories in me. I always thought you had to read them from a book.

Love,
Emma
