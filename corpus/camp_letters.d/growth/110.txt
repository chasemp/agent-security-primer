Dear Dad,

I swam in the deep part today without a life jacket. I've had the skills for a while but I always wore the jacket anyway because it made me feel safe. Today the swim counselor asked if I wanted to try without it. I said okay. And it was fine. My body knew what to do. It had known for a while. I was using the jacket as a crutch. I wonder what other crutches I'm using that I don't need anymore. That's a big thought for a Wednesday.

Miss you,
Emma
