Dear Mom and Dad,

I lost the swimming race today and I'm okay with it. I came in third out of six. The old me would have been upset for the rest of the day but today I just thought about how two weeks ago I couldn't even swim that far. I shook the winner's hand and said good race and I meant it. Being happy for someone who beat you is harder than it sounds but I think it makes you a better person. Or at least a less grumpy one.

Love,
Lily
