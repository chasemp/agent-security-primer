Dear Mom and Dad,

I want to tell you something I figured out. Being good at something right away doesn't matter as much as I thought. The kids who are good at stuff here aren't the ones who started out best. They're the ones who kept going when it was hard. I watched a kid learn to sail who tipped over eight times in one day. Eight times. He's one of the best sailors at camp now. I want to be like that. Not talented. Stubborn.

Love,
Sophie
