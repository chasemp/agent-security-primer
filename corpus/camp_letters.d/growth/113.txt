Dear Mom,

I swam the full lake crossing today with the advanced group. The counselor wasn't sure I was ready but I asked if I could try and she said okay. Halfway across my arms were so tired and the far shore looked so far away. I thought about stopping. But I could feel the water holding me up between strokes and I just kept pulling. When my feet touched the sand on the other side I stood there for a long time just breathing and being proud.

Love,
Zoe
