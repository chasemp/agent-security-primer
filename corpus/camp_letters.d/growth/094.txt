Dear Mom and Dad,

I wrote and directed a skit for cabin night. The whole thing. I came up with the idea and the characters and told everyone where to stand and what to say. It was about aliens who land at a summer camp and are confused by everything. Everyone laughed. The counselor said I should consider writing more. I never thought of myself as someone who makes things up but I guess I am. You can be something for a long time before you realize it.

Love,
Emma
