Dear Dad,

I built a fire from scratch tonight. No matches, no lighter. Flint and steel like Caleb did last week. I saw him do it and I thought if he can I can. It took me probably an hour. My hands have blisters. But when that little curl of smoke turned into a flame I yelled so loud three counselors came running because they thought something was wrong. Something was wrong. I was on fire. I mean my fire was. You know what I mean.

Love,
Marcus
