Dear Dad,

On the last night hike I was the one carrying the lantern for our group. It was dark and the trail was narrow and everyone behind me was depending on my light to see. I walked steady and called out when there were roots or rocks. Nobody tripped. We all made it to the lookout point and saw the stars. I don't know why but being responsible for the light felt like the most important thing I've done here. Showing the way for people. I want to keep doing that.

Love,
Kai
