Dear Mom,

I washed my own clothes today in the lake with soap and hung them on a line and they dried in the sun. I know at home the machine does it but there's something about doing it with your hands. I could see the dirt coming out. I wrung them and hung them and the wind did the rest. Tomorrow I'm going to wash my sleeping bag cover too. It's not exciting but it makes me feel like I can take care of myself. That feeling is exciting.

Love,
Emma
