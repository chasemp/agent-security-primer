Dear Dad,

Today a counselor asked the cabin who wanted to lead grace before dinner. Nobody raised their hand. It was that awkward silence where everyone looks at the floor. So I raised mine. I don't even know why. The words just came out and I said something about being thankful for the food and the day and being together. It wasn't fancy. But when I looked up everyone had their heads bowed and they were listening to me. That's a lot of trust for one sentence.

Love,
Aiden
