Dear Mom and Dad,

I paddled a canoe through rapids today. Small rapids but still. The water was pushing the canoe sideways and I had to steer and paddle at the same time. The canoe instructor was shouting directions and I followed every one. When we came through the other side into calm water I was soaking wet and breathing hard and my arms were burning. The instructor said nothing. He just nodded. That nod meant more than words.

Love,
Kai
