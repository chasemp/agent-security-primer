Dear Dad,

I fixed a younger kid's fishing rod today. The reel was jammed and he was about to give up. I took it apart, found the knot in the line, untangled it, and put it back together. I didn't know I could do that but my hands just figured it out. He caught two fish after that. He told everyone I fixed his rod like it was the most amazing thing. It was just a tangled line but I guess to him it was a big deal. That felt really nice.

Love,
Marcus
