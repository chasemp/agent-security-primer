Dear Mom,

I went back and tried the rock wall again today. The hard route. The one with the overhang that I couldn't get past last week. This time I studied it from the ground first and planned my moves. When I got to the overhang I reached for a hold I hadn't noticed before and it was right there. I made it to the top in half the time. The wall didn't change. I changed. I looked at the same problem with different eyes and it wasn't the same problem anymore.

Love,
Sophie
