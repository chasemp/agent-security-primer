Dear Dad,

Something happened at the lake that I want to tell you about. I saw a kid from another cabin struggling in the deep area. He wasn't drowning but he was scared and splashing a lot. I swam over and told him to hold onto my shoulder and I sidekicked us both to the shallow part. The lifeguard was already coming but I got there first. He said thank you like five times. I didn't even think I just moved. My swimming lessons actually mattered today.

Love,
Mia
