Dear Mom,

I wrote goodbye letters to three kids today and put them under their pillows. I've never done that before. Writing down that someone matters to you and giving it to them. It felt risky. What if they thought it was weird. But then Hana found hers and came and hugged me and I knew it wasn't weird. It was the opposite of weird. Telling people they matter is one of the easiest things to do and one of the hardest things to actually do. If that makes sense.

Love,
Ava
