Dear Dad,

I comforted a kid who was crying on the phone with his parents. He was in the phone booth and I could hear him through the door saying he wanted to come home. When he came out I didn't pretend I hadn't heard. I just said the first week is the hardest and it gets better. He asked how I knew and I said because I felt the same way. He sat with us at dinner. Sometimes the truest thing you can say to someone is me too.

Love,
Priya
