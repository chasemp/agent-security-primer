Dear Mom and Dad,

Today we did a blindfold hike where you hold a rope and walk through the woods with your eyes covered. I had to trust my feet and my hands and the person in front of me. I could hear everything so much better. Birds and wind and my own breathing. When we took the blindfolds off the world looked different somehow. Brighter. The counselor said sometimes you have to take away one sense to appreciate the others. I think about that a lot now.

Love,
Jordan
