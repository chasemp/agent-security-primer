Dear Mom,

I want to tell you something that might sound weird. I had a nightmare last night and I handled it by myself. I woke up scared and my heart was pounding and normally I would come to your room. But you're not here. So I lay there and took deep breaths like the counselor taught us in mindfulness and I counted the sounds I could hear outside. Crickets, wind, a distant owl. My heart slowed down and I fell back asleep. I can take care of myself in the dark now.

Love,
Sophie
