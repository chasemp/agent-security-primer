Dear Dad,

There was a storm last night and a tree branch fell on the younger kids' tent. Nobody got hurt but they were really scared. I helped Derek move the branch and then I sat with the little kids and told them stories until they calmed down. One of them fell asleep leaning on me. I didn't even think about it I just did it. Afterward Derek said I handled it like someone older than eleven. That meant a lot.

Love,
Marcus
