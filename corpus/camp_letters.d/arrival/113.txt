Dear Dad,

There are signs everywhere at camp. Wooden signs with carved letters. The paths have signs. The buildings have signs. Even the trees have little signs saying what kind of tree they are. I learned that the big one outside our cabin is a white oak. It's probably a hundred years old. I'm in Timber cabin and there's a sign on our door that says "Timber Lodge Est. 1962." That's older than Grandpa. I like that the camp has been here so long. It feels solid.

Love,
Mateo
