Dear Mom,

Something happened today that I want to remember. We were walking to the dining hall and a rainbow appeared over the lake. A full rainbow, both ends visible. Everyone stopped and stared. Even the counselors who have seen it before. The camp photographer took a picture and said she'd print copies for anyone who wants one. I want one. I'm in Birch cabin and we all stood there for a minute just looking. Camp has these moments that feel bigger than normal life.

Love,
Mia
