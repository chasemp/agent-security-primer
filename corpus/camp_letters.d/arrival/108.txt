Dear Mom and Dad,

I'm writing this in the lodge during a rainy afternoon. The fire is going in the big fireplace and someone is playing chess in the corner and there's hot cocoa. If camp was always like this I'd never leave. But mostly it's running around in the sun which is also good. I'm in Bluebell cabin and rainy days are actually my favorite so far. You slow down and talk to people and play games. My counselor taught us a card game called Spoons and it got intense.

Love,
Sadie
