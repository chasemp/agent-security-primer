Dear Mom and Dad,

The counselors do this thing at night called Roses and Thorns. Before lights out we go around and everyone says their rose (best part of the day) and their thorn (worst part). My rose today was seeing a blue heron at the lake. My thorn was the cold shower because I was last in line and the hot water ran out. I'm in Clover cabin and we all listen to each other which is nice. Some kids' thorns are homesickness. Nobody makes fun of that.

Love,
Emma
