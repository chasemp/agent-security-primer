Dear Mom,

Camp is way up in the mountains and my ears popped on the drive. The air feels different here, thinner maybe. I'm in Chickadee cabin and my counselor is named Lin. She showed us how to make our beds with hospital corners which I already forgot how to do. There are five other girls and we all picked bunks by drawing numbers from a hat. I got number three which was a middle bunk on the right side. It's fine. The stars at night are unreal though.

Love,
Priya
