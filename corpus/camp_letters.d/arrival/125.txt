Dear Dad,

Last letter for now because I'm running out of stamps. Camp is good. Really good. I know I didn't want to come and I know I complained the whole car ride. I'm sorry about that. The truth is this place is special. The people, the lake, even the lumpy mattress and the cold showers and the bug bites. It all goes together somehow. I'm in Elm cabin and I think by the time you pick me up I'm going to be different. Not a lot different. Just enough to notice.

Love,
Jordan
