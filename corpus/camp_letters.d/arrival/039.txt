Dear Dad,

First morning at camp. The wake-up bell goes off at 7 and it is LOUD. You can hear it from every cabin. Everyone groaned and nobody wanted to get up. Breakfast was pancakes and sausage and juice. Then we had flag ceremony and then cabin cleanup. Yes they make us clean. We had to sweep the floor and make our beds and everything. My cabin is Walnut and the counselor inspects it every morning. It's like the army but with more mosquitoes.

Love,
Tyler
