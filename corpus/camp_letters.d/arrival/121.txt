Dear Mom,

There's a tradition here where new campers tie a ribbon to the Welcome Tree on their first day. It's this big old tree by the entrance covered in hundreds of ribbons from years and years of campers. Some are faded and some are new. I tied a blue one. My counselor said when I come back next year I can look for it. I'm in Rosemary cabin and all six of us tied our ribbons next to each other. I hope they stay there all year.

Love,
Sophie
