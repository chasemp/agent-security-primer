Dear Mom,

The bus smelled like cheese and someone's bag leaked juice on the floor. But camp is pretty cool so far. My cabin is called Hawk and there are six of us. I got the bottom bunk by the window. The girl above me is named Zoe and she brought like ten books. Our counselor Tamara did a name game and I already forgot half the names. There's a fire pit outside the main lodge and they said we roast marshmallows on Fridays. Can you send my blue hoodie? I only brought the thin one.

Love,
Emma
