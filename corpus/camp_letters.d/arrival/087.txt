Dear Mom,

We had a thunderstorm last night and the power went out. The counselors brought out lanterns and candles and it was actually really cozy. We told stories in the cabin until the storm passed. One girl told a story about a ghost camp across the lake and everyone pretended not to be scared. I'm in Elderberry cabin and the rain on the roof was so loud we had to shout. When the power came back we all kind of wished it hadn't. The lanterns were nicer.

Love,
Zoe
