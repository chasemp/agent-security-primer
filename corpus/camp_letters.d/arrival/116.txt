Dear Dad,

The sunset here goes on forever. The sky turns orange then pink then purple and it takes like an hour. At home you barely notice it but here we all stop and watch. We sit on the hill by the flagpole and someone usually starts singing quietly. I'm in Osprey cabin and sunset is right before evening program. Sometimes I wish we could skip the program and just keep watching the sky. My counselor says I sound like a poet. I'm just describing what I see.

Love,
Tyler
