Dear Mom and Dad,

The weirdest thing about camp is eating every meal with the same people. At home I eat dinner with you guys and lunch at school with my friends. Here I eat everything with my cabin. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, even snack time. I know exactly what everyone orders and who eats fast and who picks at their food. I'm in Tanager cabin with six kids. We're like a little family already. My counselor Will calls us the Tanager Tribe. It's corny but it works.

Love,
