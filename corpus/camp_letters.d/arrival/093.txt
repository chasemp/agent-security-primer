Dear Mom,

The night sky here is different from home. I can see the Milky Way. Actually see it. Not just a few stars but a whole stripe of white across the sky. My counselor brought us outside after lights out to look at it and pointed out constellations. I found the Big Dipper by myself. I'm in Moonrise cabin which is a perfect name for it. There are seven of us and we all lay in the grass looking up. Nobody talked for like five minutes which at camp is basically a miracle.

Love,
Emma
