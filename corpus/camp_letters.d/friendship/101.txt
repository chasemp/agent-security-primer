Dear Mom and Dad,

Sage finally performed at the talent show. She played guitar and sang a song she wrote herself. Her voice was shaking at first but by the second verse she was strong. Our whole cabin stood up. Not because anyone told us to. We just did. She cried after and said she never would have done it without us. I think we all felt proud. Like her bravery belonged to all of us a little.

Love,
Zoe
