Dear Mom,

I read my mailbox notes on the bus ride home. There were nine of them. Each one made me feel something different. Aiden's made me laugh. Sophie's made me think. Mateo's made me cry a little but don't tell anyone. I folded them all up and put them in the ziplock bag with my friendship bracelet and my pinecone and my pressed four-leaf clover. My bag of proof that it was all real.

Love,
