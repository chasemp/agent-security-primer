Dear Dad,

Wes brought his binoculars to the campfire and let everyone take turns looking at the moon. You could see craters. He was so happy showing everyone. He pointed out every feature like he was giving a tour. When it was my turn he said "look to the left, that dark spot is the Sea of Tranquility." I think Wes is happiest when he's sharing what he knows. Most people just want someone to listen.
