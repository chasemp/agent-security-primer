Dear Dad,

This morning I woke up before everyone and went to the lake by myself. Then Ava showed up because she woke up early too. We watched the mist come off the water and didn't say anything for a long time. A heron flew across the lake. Ava whispered "that was magic." It's rare to find someone you can be quiet with. Most people feel like they have to fill the silence.

Love,
Sophie
