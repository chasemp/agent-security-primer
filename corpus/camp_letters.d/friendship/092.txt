Dear Dad,

Ava and I found a four-leaf clover today on the path to the arts cabin. We argued about who should keep it. She said me because I found it. I said her because she pointed to the patch of clover first. We ended up pressing it in a book and deciding we'd each take turns keeping it. Six months each. Love,
