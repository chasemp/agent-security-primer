Dear Mom,

I woke up in the middle of the night and the rain had stopped. It was so quiet I could hear everyone breathing. Then the drip from the roof bucket started going slower and slower. Plink. Plink. Plink. Like a clock winding down. I lay there and listened and felt weirdly happy. Then I fell back asleep and when I woke up it was raining again. But that quiet moment was something.

Miss you,
Noah
