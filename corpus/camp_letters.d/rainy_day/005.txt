Dear Dad,

The rain stopped for like an hour and we all ran outside like escaped prisoners. Everything was muddy and the field was a swamp but nobody cared. We played capture the flag in the mud and by the end you couldn't tell what team anyone was on because we were all brown. The counselors made us hose off before going inside. It was the most fun I've had all week and we didn't use any equipment or anything. Just mud.

Love,
Noah
