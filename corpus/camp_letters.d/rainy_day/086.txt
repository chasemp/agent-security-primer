Dear Mom,

A salamander got into our cabin. It came in through the gap under the door where the rain water seeps in. Zoe found it under her bunk and named it Puddles before anyone else could say anything. We watched it for a while and then the counselor made us put it back outside. Zoe was sad. I told her Puddles would be happier in the rain. She said she knew but still.

Love,
Sophie
