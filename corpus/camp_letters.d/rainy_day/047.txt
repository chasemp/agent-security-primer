Dear Mom,

Today I just stared out the window for a really long time. The rain makes these patterns on the glass that look like tiny rivers. I watched one drop race another drop to the bottom and I picked a winner. The one I picked lost. My luck lately. Emma asked what I was doing and I said thinking. She said about what. I said about when it's going to stop raining. She didn't have an answer.

Love,
Lily
