Dear Mom and Dad,

It stopped. It actually stopped. I woke up and the sun was coming through the window and I just stared at it for a minute. Then everyone woke up and we ran outside in our pajamas. The ground was still muddy and the air smelled like wet leaves and everything was dripping from the trees. But the sky was blue. Real blue. After a week of rain the world looks brand new. I know I complained a lot but I'll remember this week for a long time. The leaky roof and the Uno and the hot chocolate and all of it. Maybe especially all of it.

Love,
Noah
