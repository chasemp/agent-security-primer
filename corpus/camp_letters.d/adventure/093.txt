Dear Dad,

We watched the sunrise from the canoe this morning. Left the dock at 5:30 and paddled to the middle of the lake. The water was perfectly flat like a mirror. You could see the sky and the trees reflected upside down. Then the sun came up over the ridge and everything turned gold. The fish were jumping. You could hear them splash. Mia and I just sat there in the canoe and didn't paddle for a while. Just floated.

Love,
Ava
