Dear Dad,

We did a solo sit today. That's where you go into the woods by yourself and sit alone for thirty minutes. No talking, no moving, just sitting and watching. I was nervous at first but then I noticed SO MUCH. A chipmunk came right up to my shoe. A woodpecker was hammering on a tree above me. Ants were carrying a leaf that was bigger than all of them put together. The forest is busy when you stop and look.

Love,
Chloe
