Dear Mom and Dad,

We did the zip line at night. NIGHT ZIP LINE. They strung up lights along the cable and you fly through the dark with just these little fairy lights around you. You can't see the ground at all. It feels like you're flying through space. My stomach dropped even harder than daytime because you can't judge the speed. When I got to the platform at the bottom my legs were shaking. I've never felt anything like that.

Love,
Mateo
