Dear Dad,

Nature walk today and we found a snake. A real live garter snake just sitting on a rock in the sun. The naturalist picked it up and showed us how to hold it. I held it and it was dry and smooth not slimy like I thought. It flicked its tongue on my hand. Some kids wouldn't touch it but I let it wrap around my fingers. It was squeezing but not hard. Just holding on. Snakes are actually pretty cool. Don't tell my sister I said that.
