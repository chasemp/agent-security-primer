Dear Dad,

Horseback riding on the beach trail today. I didn't even know camp had a beach trail. The horses walk right along the sand and you can hear the waves. My horse was named Scout and he kept trying to eat the tall grass on the side of the trail. The instructor said to pull the reins up but he was persistent. We trotted along the water's edge and the sand kicked up behind us. It felt like a cowboy movie.

Love,
Aiden
