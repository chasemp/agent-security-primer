Dear Mom and Dad,

Tomorrow I come home. I'm packing my bag right now and everything smells like campfire and sunscreen and lake water. I have thirteen bug bites, rope burns on both hands, a scraped knee, sore shoulders, and a bruise on my shin from the canoe. I've never been this tired or this happy. Camp changed me. I stood on edges and jumped. I tried things that scared me and they became my favorite things. Save me a spot on the couch. I have a lot of stories to tell.

Love,
Caleb
