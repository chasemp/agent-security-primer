Dear Dad,

We did the final hike today. Everyone hiked together to Sunset Ridge for the last evening. The trail was easier than Bald Mountain but longer and we sang camp songs the whole way. When we got to the ridge the sun was going down and the sky was orange and purple and pink. Someone started the camp song and everyone joined in. Some people were crying but happy crying. I got a lump in my throat. This place is special.

Love,
Jordan
