Dear Mom and Dad,

Water went everywhere. We were all laughing when we came up. Then we floated on our backs in a circle looking up at the clouds. Nobody wanted to get out. The lifeguard had to blow her whistle three times. I'm going to miss this lake.

Love,
Sophie
