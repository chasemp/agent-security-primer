Dear Mom and Dad,

We hiked through the gorge today. The walls are rock on both sides and they're so tall you have to look straight up to see the sky. There's a stream running through the bottom and we had to hop between rocks to stay dry. I only got one shoe wet. The echo in there is amazing. When you clap it bounces back like five times. Emma clapped a rhythm and the gorge played it back to us. Nature is the coolest instrument.

Love,
Lily
