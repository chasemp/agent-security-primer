Dear Mom,

I'm writing this on the bus home. Camp is getting smaller in the back window. Ava and I promised to look at Vega tonight from our own houses and think of each other. I still have Bear in my trunk from when you sent him and I'm glad you did. I'm different than when I got on this bus two weeks ago. I don't know how to explain it. I'm still me but I'm also someone who swam to the deep dock and did a talent show and has a friend named Ava who knows the stars. That's more than I was before.

Love,
Mia
